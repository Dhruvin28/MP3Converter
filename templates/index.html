YouTube to MP3

Free & runs locally on your machine

What this does

This tool downloads audio from any YouTube video or playlist and converts it to high-quality MP3. All tracks are bundled into a single ZIP file you can save anywhere. Everything runs on your local server — nothing is uploaded to a third-party service.

Features

320 kbps MP3
Best available audio extracted & converted
Full playlists
Download every track, numbered in order
ZIP download
All files packed, one click to save
Local only
No uploads, no accounts, no tracking

How it works

1

Copy a YouTube URL — a single video or an entire playlist

2

Paste it in the box and click Convert (or press Enter)

3

Watch the live log while audio is downloaded & converted

4

Click Download ZIP to save all tracks to your computer
